Annapurna India

Annapurna India is a new website that aims to help solve the problem of hunger and food waste in India. Every day, a lot of good food gets thrown away because there's too much of it or it's not distributed properly. Our website connects people who have extra food with organizations that can give it to people who need it, like NGOs and charities. The website is easy to use. People who have extra food can upload information about it, and NGOs and charities can find that information and arrange to pick up the food. This way, we can make sure that less food goes to waste and more people get the food they need. Imagine a website called Annapurna India that's like a superhero fighting against hunger and food waste. It's all about stopping good food from being thrown away and making sure it gets to people who need it most. Here's how it works: if there's a restaurant, store, or farm with extra food they can't sell, they can go on the Annapurna India website and say, "Hey, we have food to donate!" Then, NGOs and charities who help hungry people can see that and come pick up the food to give to those who need it. With Annapurna India, we're using technology to make it easier to share food and help people who don't have enough to eat. Join us in making a difference and building a better future where no one goes hungry.
